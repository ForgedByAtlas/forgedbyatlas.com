How it works

One setup. Then it just sounds like you.

  1. 1

    Paste your writing once

    Drop in five or more samples of your professional writing and five or more of your casual writing. This is a one-time setup. The extension uses them to learn how you actually write.

  2. 2

    Type what you want to say

    Give it the context (an email you got) and a short instruction (what you want to say back). Pick Professional or Personal voice.

    It writes the reply the way you would have. Copy it, paste it wherever you need it, and send. No editing needed.

FAQ

Do you store what I write?

No. The message content you send for generation is never stored on our servers. It's processed to produce your reply and then it's gone.

The only writing we keep are your setup samples, and those are saved in your browser's Chrome sync storage, not in our database. They stay with you.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT writes in a generic, average voice because it's trained on everyone. MyVoice.AI is matched to your writing specifically, using the samples you provide. The output sounds like you, which means you can send it as-is instead of rewriting it to not sound like a robot.

What's the free tier?

Five generations per day, resetting daily, with no card required. Both Professional and Personal voices are included. If you need more than five a day, Pro removes the limit.

Where are my writing samples kept?

In your browser's Chrome sync storage. That means they're tied to your Chrome profile, not sitting in our database. We don't have a copy.

How do I sign in?

With a magic link. You enter your email and we send you a sign-in link. No password to create or forget.
